TBH · AI Adoption Strategy Executive Summary
Overview
Why This Plan, Why Now
Guiding PrincipleRationale
Listen before building Survey data drives every tool decision. No assumptions.
Right tool, right job n8n/Make for routine tasks. Claude/ChatGPT for knowledge work. Don't mix.
Employee-led adoption People adopt tools they helped choose. Surveys give staff a real voice.
Measure everything Baseline captured before rollout. Impact proven with data, not perception.
Improve continuously Phase 4 is not the end — every cycle feeds the next sprint.
The Problem
Scattered AI usage, no governance, shadow IT spend, inconsistent output quality, no baseline to measure against.
The Outcome
One standardised toolset per task type, measurable productivity gains, employee confidence up, and a quarterly improvement loop.
Timeline
14-week structured rollout → ongoing quarterly improvement cycle from Week 15+

TBH · AI Adoption Strategy Phase 3 · Week 14 Decision Gate
Go / No-Go Decision Gate
Review pilot data at end of Week 14
✓ Go
≥4 of 7 metrics hit threshold
Proceed to company-wide rollout at Phase 4. Lock in tool subscriptions and comms plan.
⚠ Conditional
2–3 metrics hit threshold
Extend pilot 2 weeks. Fix specific blockers — training gap, workflow config, or tool selection. Re-measure.
✗ No-Go
Fewer than 2 metrics
Return to Phase 2. Re-evaluate tool selection, re-run onboarding. Investigate root cause before re-piloting.
Phase 3 → Phase 4 Handoff Checklist
☐ Go/No-Go decision documented with data
☐ Tool subscriptions confirmed (if Go)
☐ Pilot learnings converted to backlog items
☐ Phase 4 improvement survey designed & ready
☐ Workflow + AI Champions confirmed per team
☐ Company-wide rollout comms drafted

Quarterly Improvement Cycle
01 Deploy 10-question survey — min 80% response target
02 Compare metrics vs. previous quarter. Top 3 wins + top 3 pain points.
03 Build improvement backlog — rank by impact × effort, assign owner + date.
04 Implement. Share "You said, we did" summary with the team.
